# MiniPay Payout Ops Checklist

This checklist covers the manual, non-code steps that remain outside the repo for monthly reward claims.

## Project Leader Claim Checklist

- Confirm the Project Leader controls the MiniPay wallet that will receive the payout.
- Confirm the payout Mini App is reachable from the same MiniPay account used for claiming.
- Claim the rewards before the next month's distribution window starts.
- Save the payout confirmation screenshot, transaction hash, and claim date for audit records.
- Keep the claimant identity consistent across Talent App, MiniPay, and the project submission materials.

## Evidence To Keep

- Project Leader MiniPay wallet address or alias.
- Claim transaction hash.
- Claim confirmation screenshot.
- The month and program name for the claim.

## Booster Tracking

Proof of Ship and live MiniPay readiness are score boosters, not base blockers. Keep the following handy if reviewers ask for them:

- Deployed Mini App URL.
- Screenshot or recording of the MiniPay connect flow.
- Screenshot of the MiniPay-specific Celo / USDT path in the app.
- Any Proof of Ship submission or acceptance artifact you receive.

## Notes

- The current frontend already contains MiniPay detection and auto-connect support.
- Do not wait until the last day of the next reward cycle. Treat the claim window as an ops deadline owned by the Project Leader.
